About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Engineering at Seoul National University, specializing in Technology Management, Economics, and Policy. My research focuses on applying advanced data-driven methods to analyze technological evolution and global competitiveness. I leverage econometrics, machine learning, and transformer-based architectures to uncover patterns in large-scale technological datasets.

Research Interests

  • Exploration of knowledge landscapes using language models, including LLMs
  • Measures of innovation and scientific progress
  • Prediction of future breakthroughs and emerging fields
  • Product evolution and technology complexity
  • Entrepreneurship and Innovation
